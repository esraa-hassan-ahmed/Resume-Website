About

Dr. Mourady was introduced to the industry and became an established member of the Retail Real Estate community by joining Arabian Centres in 2012 as online marketing manager. His responsibilities included the promotion, management, development, delivery and evaluation of the rapidly changing and increasing use of online technology - keeping abreast of industry trends and challenges and providing business intelligence to the company. During this year, he led the team to create a unified online platform, create and manage 13 new websites, increase the social media fans & followers from 40,762 to 1.4 million effective followers. Under Mourady’s leadership and significant contribution, Arabian Centres won 2 awards for Best in Technology from MECSC.
